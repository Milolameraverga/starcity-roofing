El Paso Roof Repair, We Find the Cause, Not Just the Drip

Not every roofing problem needs a full replacement. Star City Roofing has handled hundreds of repairs across El Paso, from pipe boots in Woodhaven (79934) to flashing failures in Cielo Vista, valley leaks in Horizon City to wind damage in the Upper Valley.

The first thing we do is find where the water is actually getting in, not where it's showing up inside. Common Roof Repairs We Handle in El Paso

  • Pipe boot / plumbing vent flashing replacement: One of the most common roof repairs in El Paso. The rubber boot around pipe vents degrades rapidly under UV exposure, cracking and pulling away from the pipe. A failed pipe boot lets water run straight into your attic along the pipe. Repair is fast and effective.
  • Missing, cracked, or curling shingles: Single-shingle or small-section repairs when the rest of the roof is in good condition. We match the existing profile and color as closely as possible.
  • Ridge cap failure: Ridge caps take the most wind exposure of any roof component. In El Paso's afternoon winds, lifted or missing ridge caps are a frequent call. We replace with matching material and properly nail and seal to prevent recurrence.
  • Chimney and masonry flashing: Flashing that seals a chimney or masonry wall to the roof is a common failure point, especially on older El Paso homes. Failed step flashing and counter flashing allow water to run directly into wall cavities.
  • Valley metal repair or replacement: Metal valleys that have rusted, separated, or been improperly installed are a major source of leaks in El Paso. We replace sections or full valleys with new material and proper underlayment.
  • Skylight flashing failure: Many El Paso homes have skylights that develop leaks at the curb or deck flashing. We reseal, re-flash, or replace the surrounding flashing assembly as needed.
  • Fascia and soffit damage repair: Wind-driven moisture and animal activity can damage soffit and fascia boards, especially in older El Paso construction. We repair or replace damaged sections and prime and seal the new wood before we leave.
  • Emergency tarping after storm damage: If your roof has suffered sudden damage during a storm, we can provide emergency tarping to protect your home from further water intrusion while a permanent repair or replacement is scheduled.

When Repair Makes Sense vs. Full Replacement

We'll always give you our honest professional recommendation. Generally, a repair makes sense when:

  • The damage is isolated to one or two areas
  • The overall shingle condition is good (minimal granule loss, no widespread cracking)
  • The roof is under 15 years old
  • The decking is sound beneath the damaged area
  • You haven't had repeated repairs in the same area

Replacement becomes the better investment when the roof is 20+ years old, damage is widespread, you've repaired the same area multiple times, or the cost of repair approaches 30–40% of replacement cost. We'll walk you through the math and let you decide.

Roof Repair FAQs, El Paso, TX

How much does roof repair cost in El Paso? +
Repair costs depend on the type and extent of the problem. Minor repairs (single pipe boot, a few shingles, small flashing patch) typically run $200–$600. More involved repairs (valley replacement, chimney re-flashing, multiple areas) may run $600–$2,500. We provide fully itemized, no-obligation estimates before any work begins.
How fast can you respond to a roof leak in El Paso? +
For standard repair estimates, we aim for same-day visits. For active leaks during or after a storm, call us directly at (915) 740-5961, we prioritize emergency situations. Emergency tarping is typically available next-day or sooner to stop water intrusion while a permanent repair is scheduled.
Can I repair just one section of my El Paso roof? +
Yes, in most cases. If the rest of your roof is in good condition, targeted repairs are absolutely the right call. We're upfront about this, if a section repair will serve you well for another 5–10 years, that's what we'll recommend. If the overall roof condition suggests replacement is imminent, we'll tell you that too so you can plan accordingly.
My roof is leaking but I can't figure out where, can you find it? +
This is one of the most common repair calls we get. Water travels along rafters and decking before dripping, so the visible wet spot inside your home is often far from the actual entry point. We perform a systematic inspection of the most common entry points, pipe boots, valleys, ridges, wall flashings, and can often trace the source on the first visit. In difficult cases, a water test confirms the exact location.
Do you offer a warranty on roof repairs in El Paso? +
Yes. We back our repairs with a workmanship warranty. If a repair we performed fails due to our workmanship within the warranty period, we'll return and fix it at no charge. We'll discuss the specific warranty terms during your estimate, they vary based on the type and scope of the repair.
