Roofing the Upper Valley — El Paso's Rio Grande Corridor

The Upper Valley of El Paso runs along the Rio Grande north and northwest of the city, encompassing the communities of Canutillo, Vinton, Berino, and the historic agricultural neighborhoods that give this area its distinct character. Properties here tend to be larger, the pace is quieter, and the mix of housing includes everything from decades-old adobe and masonry homes to newer custom builds and ranches.

Roofing Considerations Unique to the Upper Valley

  • Adobe and masonry homes — Many Upper Valley properties are older adobe or brick construction with parapet walls and built-up flat roofs. These require specialized treatment — including proper flashing at parapet caps, elastomeric coatings for the flat decking, and metal coping. We know how to work on these structures correctly.
  • Agricultural moisture exposure — The proximity to irrigation canals and agricultural operations creates higher ambient humidity than most El Paso areas during certain seasons. This can accelerate roof deck moisture damage if flashing or membranes are compromised.
  • Wind from the river corridor — The Rio Grande creates natural wind corridors that can drive sustained high winds through the Upper Valley, particularly in spring. Ridge cap failures and wind-lifted shingles are more common here than in more sheltered parts of the city.
  • Aging structures and deferred maintenance — Some Upper Valley properties have had roofing maintenance deferred for years. We assess these situations honestly and help you prioritize what needs immediate attention versus what can wait — protecting your investment without forcing unnecessary work.

Upper Valley Roofing — FAQ

Do you work on adobe and flat-roofed homes in the Upper Valley? +
Yes. Adobe and masonry homes with parapet walls and built-up flat roofs are common in the Upper Valley, and we have experience repairing and re-coating these systems. For flat sections, we apply elastomeric reflective coatings that seal the surface and reduce heat gain. For parapet walls, we address flashing failures at the cap and base, which are the most common leak points on these structures.
How far do you travel for jobs in the Upper Valley? +
We travel throughout the Upper Valley corridor including Canutillo, Vinton, Berino, Anthony, and surrounding communities. We typically offer same-day estimates for all Upper Valley locations. Call (915) 740-5961 and we'll confirm availability for your specific address.
What is the best roofing material for the Upper Valley climate? +
For pitched shingle roofs, Owens Corning Duration Series with Class 4 impact resistance performs best in the Upper Valley — resistant to hail, high UV, and wind uplift. For flat and low-slope roofs common on adobe-style homes, a high-quality elastomeric acrylic coating applied over a clean membrane surface provides the best combination of durability, waterproofing, and solar reflectance.
Can you help with commercial roofing in Canutillo or Vinton? +
Yes. We service commercial flat roofs throughout the Upper Valley with coating applications, membrane repairs, and complete replacements where needed. Our flat roof coating services are particularly well-suited to agricultural and light commercial buildings in this area.
