Areas We Serve

Every neighborhood in El Paso has different roofing problems. Click your area to see what's most common where you live and what we've done there.

Northeast El Paso

ZIP: 79934, 79936, Star City's home base

Woodhaven, Sunrise Acres, Mission Hills, Montwood, Cielo Vista. Hail damage, UV degradation, and aging T-lock shingles are the most common issues. Our shop is right here on Dyer St.

East El Paso

ZIP: 79928, 79938, Zaragoza, Vista Hills, Yarbrough

Large subdivisions built in the 1980s–2000s with roofs now reaching end-of-life. Hail damage, inadequate ventilation, and aging pipe boot seals are frequent problems.

East El Paso Roofing →

West El Paso

ZIP: 79912, 79922, 79932, Coronado, Mesa Hills, Kern Place

Established neighborhoods with older organic mat shingles, Franklin Mountain wind exposure, and multi-layer roofs overdue for tear-off. Strong demand, growing Star City presence.

West El Paso Roofing →

Upper Valley

Canutillo, Vinton, Berino, Anthony TX, Westway

Adobe and masonry homes with traditional flat roofs, agricultural moisture exposure, Rio Grande wind corridors. Flat roof coating and repair are especially common needs here.

Upper Valley Roofing →

Horizon City

East of El Paso on I-10, fastest-growing suburb

Fast-growing suburb with builder-grade shingles now reaching 15–20 years. Significant hail corridor exposure east of El Paso. Lots of HOA neighborhoods with specific color requirements.

Horizon City Roofing →

Socorro, TX

ZIP: 79927, Mission Bend, Del Valle, Sparks, Loma Blanca

Lower Valley community with older housing stock, aging flat and built-up roofs, and moisture intrusion from storm season. Many homes are approaching or past original roof lifespan.
