About Star City
Roofing

El Paso born and raised. We've been protecting El Paso homes since day one — with honest work, quality materials, and neighbors you can trust.

God. Family. Roofing.

Star City Roofing was built on simple values — do the job right, treat people fairly, and stand behind your work. We're a locally owned El Paso roofing company, not a national franchise. When you call us, you get a real person who lives in this city and cares about its homes.

Over 7 years of roofing in El Paso has taught us everything about what this climate does to roofs — the intense UV, the heat cycling, the monsoon rains, and the hail. We know what materials hold up and what shortcuts fail. That knowledge is what makes us different.
