Signs You Need a Roof Replacement in El Paso

  • Roof is 18–20+ years old, El Paso's UV accelerates shingle wear faster than most Texas cities
  • Shingles missing, cracked, or curling in multiple areas
  • Heavy granule loss collecting in your gutters
  • Soft spots or visible sagging in the decking
  • You've repaired the same area more than once and it keeps leaking
  • Hail or wind damage across a large section of the roof
  • Water stains on your ceilings or attic framing

What Does a Roof Replacement Cost in El Paso?

Here's the honest answer: it depends on your roof's size, pitch, what shingles you choose, and how much decking needs to come out. Based on what we've done across El Paso:

Roof Replacement FAQs, El Paso, TX

How much does a roof replacement cost in El Paso? +
In El Paso, a typical roof replacement runs $7,000–$20,000+ depending on roof size, pitch, materials, and decking condition. A standard 1,500–2,000 sq ft home with architectural shingles typically costs $9,000–$14,000. We provide free, itemized estimates with no obligation.
How long does a roof replacement take? +
Most El Paso residential replacements are done in 1–2 days. Larger or more complex roofs may take 2–3 days. We work efficiently, your home is protected fast.
What shingles are best for El Paso's heat? +
We recommend Class 4 impact-resistant architectural shingles with high solar reflectance ratings. GAF Timberline HDZ and Owens Corning Duration are top performers in El Paso's climate, they resist UV degradation, hold up to hail, and come with strong manufacturer warranties.
Will insurance cover my roof replacement? +
If the damage was caused by hail, wind, or another covered peril, yes, your homeowners insurance may cover a full or partial replacement. We help you document the damage and can work alongside your adjuster to make the process smoother.
Do I need to be home during the replacement? +
Not necessarily. We handle everything, including debris cleanup and a nail sweep. We do recommend a final walkthrough with you at the end, but we can coordinate around your schedule.
