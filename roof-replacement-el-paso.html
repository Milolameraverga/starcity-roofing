Our roof replacements include full tear-off of old materials, inspection and repair of the decking, installation of ice and water shield, proper underlayment, and premium shingles rated for extreme desert conditions. We don't cut corners, and our workmanship is backed by a solid warranty.

Signs You Need a Roof Replacement in El Paso

  • Roof is 20+ years old (El Paso heat accelerates wear)
  • Multiple areas of missing, cracked, or curling shingles
  • Granules collecting in gutters after rain
  • Visible sagging or soft spots in the decking
  • Recurring leaks that repairs haven't fixed permanently
  • Storm or hail damage covering a large portion of the roof
  • Interior water damage or staining on ceilings

What's Included in Our El Paso Roof Replacement

  • Complete removal and disposal of old roofing materials
  • Decking inspection — repair or replace damaged boards
  • Install synthetic underlayment for superior moisture barrier
  • Ice & water shield in valleys and vulnerable areas
  • Premium architectural shingles (GAF, Owens Corning, CertainTeed)
  • New ridge cap, flashing, and pipe boots
  • Full cleanup — magnet sweep for nails, haul away all debris
  • Before & after photo documentation

Roof Replacement Cost in El Paso, TX

The cost of a roof replacement in El Paso depends on the size of your roof, the pitch (steepness), the materials you choose, and whether any decking needs replacement. As a rough guide:

  • Small home (under 1,500 sq ft) — typically $7,000–$11,000
  • Average El Paso home (1,500–2,500 sq ft) — typically $9,000–$16,000
  • Larger home (2,500+ sq ft) — typically $14,000–$22,000+

These are estimates — the only way to get an accurate price is a free on-site inspection. We provide detailed, itemized quotes with no hidden fees. The number we give you is the number you pay.

Roof Replacement FAQs — El Paso, TX

How much does a roof replacement cost in El Paso? +
In El Paso, a typical roof replacement runs $7,000–$20,000+ depending on roof size, pitch, materials, and decking condition. A standard 1,500–2,000 sq ft home with architectural shingles typically costs $9,000–$14,000. We provide free, itemized estimates with no obligation.
How long does a roof replacement take? +
Most El Paso residential replacements are done in 1–2 days. Larger or more complex roofs may take 2–3 days. We work efficiently — your home is protected fast.
What shingles are best for El Paso's heat? +
We recommend Class 4 impact-resistant architectural shingles with high solar reflectance ratings. GAF Timberline HDZ and Owens Corning Duration are top performers in El Paso's climate — they resist UV degradation, hold up to hail, and come with strong manufacturer warranties.
Will insurance cover my roof replacement? +
If the damage was caused by hail, wind, or another covered peril, yes — your homeowners insurance may cover a full or partial replacement. We help you document the damage and can work alongside your adjuster to make the process smoother.
Do I need to be home during the replacement? +
Not necessarily. We handle everything, including debris cleanup and a magnetic nail sweep. We do recommend a final walkthrough with you at the end, but we can coordinate around your schedule.
