Common Questions

Roof Replacement FAQ

How much does a roof replacement cost in El Paso? +
A full residential roof replacement in El Paso typically runs $8,000 to $18,000 depending on roof size, pitch, shingle type, and condition of the decking. We provide a free, detailed estimate before any work starts so you know exactly what you're paying for. No surprises.
How long does a roof replacement take? +
Most standard residential replacements in El Paso are completed in one to two days. Larger homes, complex roof lines, or significant decking damage can add time. We give you a realistic timeline upfront and stick to it.
Do I actually need a full replacement or can repairs fix it? +
We'll give you an honest answer on this. If your roof has localized damage and the rest of the system is sound, repairs can extend its life significantly. If the shingles are aged out, the decking is compromised, or you've had repeated leaks, replacement is the better long-term investment. We don't push replacements when repairs will do the job.
Do you work with insurance for storm damage replacements? +
Yes. We document the damage, work alongside your insurance adjuster, and help you understand what your policy covers. We can't guarantee claim outcomes, but we make sure the damage is properly documented so your adjuster has everything they need to process the claim correctly.
What shingle brands do you use? +
We work with GAF and Owens Corning shingles, two of the most trusted names in the industry. Both offer strong manufacturer warranties and perform well in El Paso's climate. We'll walk you through the options and help you pick what fits your home and budget.
