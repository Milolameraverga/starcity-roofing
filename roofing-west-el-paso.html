Why West El Paso Homes Need Attention Now

West El Paso has a large population of homes built in the 1960s through 1990s. This means a significant number of roofs are at or beyond their expected lifespan. Even if they're not actively leaking, these older systems may be:

  • Carrying multiple layers of shingles that exceed code limits and hold extra weight
  • Built with organic mat shingles that absorb moisture and degrade from the inside
  • Missing modern underlayment and ice-and-water barrier protection
  • Suffering from deteriorated flashing at chimneys, skylights, and parapets
  • Lacking proper ridge ventilation, which accelerates UV damage to shingles from below

A free inspection from Star City Roofing will tell you exactly where your roof stands, with no pressure and no obligation to proceed.

West El Paso Roofing, FAQ

How much does roof replacement cost in West El Paso? +
West El Paso homes vary significantly in size and roof complexity. A standard 1,500–2,500 sq ft home typically runs $9,000–$16,000 for a full replacement with Owens Corning Duration shingles. Larger or more complex roofs, steep pitches, or significant decking damage will increase the total. We provide fully itemized estimates at no cost or obligation.
Do you work on older homes in Kern Place and Coronado Hills? +
Absolutely. We have experience with older West El Paso homes, including those with multiple shingle layers, original flashing systems, and non-standard roof configurations. We assess each home's specific situation and build a scope of work that addresses the structural realities of the existing construction, not just a one-size-fits-all approach.
Does wind near the Franklin Mountains affect roofs in West El Paso? +
Yes, West El Paso's proximity to the Franklin Mountains creates channeled wind events, particularly in spring. High winds can lift ridge caps, pop nails, and peel shingles at vulnerable edges. After significant wind events, a quick inspection can catch damage early and prevent water intrusion. We offer free post-storm inspections for West El Paso homeowners.
Can I get an Owens Corning warranty in West El Paso? +
Yes. As an Owens Corning Preferred Contractor, we can offer extended TotalProtection and System Plus warranties that are only available through certified contractors. These cover both the materials and our workmanship, something you won't get from uncertified roofers using the same shingles purchased at a home center.
